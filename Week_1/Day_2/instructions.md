## Users have account

## Users can spend money

    - Your account balance reduces
    - Your transaction history increase

## Users can receive money

    - Your account balance increases
    - Your transaction history increases

## We are going to create 3 functions

## Function1: Receiving money

1. Takes an account number
2. Money incoming

## Function2: Updating transaction history

1. Takes an account number
2. Updates transaction history

## Function3: Sending money

1. Takes an account number
2. Money outgoing
